Precision Digital Diagnostics and HealthTech

We develop aptamers and integrate them into platforms to produce next-generation digital diagnostics solutions. Our expertise lends itself to develop novel diagnostic solutions and enter various markets of interest. Our business is based on two key components: 1) development of novel aptamers to address the diagnostic need and 2) integration of developed aptamers onto biosensor platforms to provide a total diagnostic solution. The two components lead to a successful market entry and product launch.

Our Technology

Aptamers are smart DNA sequences. We make aptamers that fit the biomarker target and solve the disease puzzle.
